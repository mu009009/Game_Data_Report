Summary
Generally Speaking, based on the GDP change data, game marketing data, game sales data, and the game publisher sales data, we could see that for the different area, the game market and games sales has a lot of different.

Japan has become the smallest market all over the world, and the USA and Europe have the similar size of the game market.

This can also somehow explain that the game market does not influence by the GDP too much, cause the publisher in this market is more influenced by the global preference, but not the preference or consumption habits from one country area.

What's more, this also leaves the inspiration, that if a publisher would like to join this market, it is wise to use a global strategy to develop their companies. And if they have sufficient resource, it is better to design a game for long term market round, this benefit much more than those games has only a short life.
