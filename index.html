About this Project
This project is mainly about Games. As a game player, and who want to join the game products as my career in the future. I hope to understand and learn more about the game market. Thus, I try to connect the game GDP data with game sales to reveal the stories and conclusions behind the data.

Enterprise Stories
Below is the part to show the data about game enterprise from all over the world. Cause enterprise is a big part for finance and also will have a huge influence to the relevant market, thus I decided to look into the data to see how many products the produced, and how many copies they sold.
Based on the two combination maps above, the enterprise in different areas has done similar sales.
However, the data in the sales map part and the data in the weekly sales analysis part both show that the game market is different in different countries. Especially the game market in Japan, it is a dying market.

At first, the number of Japan Game Enterprise is similar to the Game Enterprise based in USA, both has around 195 companies, and about 30 more comapnies than the Europe Area. Besides, Japanese companies also have sold as much as USA companies did in the past years and more than the sum of Europe's. And the Japanese company, Nintendo has sold the most game copies, 625.4 million of copies, as about the top in Europe, Electronic Arts have done 602.6, while the top in the USA, Activision has only done 504.7 million copies sales.

Thus I got the conclusion that in fact, game companies are using some global strategy to save their sales. Cause their target users of their products are from all over the world, and they are competing in the same global game market, not only the local area. This will explain why those Japanese Companies still do the similar sale as the western companies did, while their own game marketing is decreasing.

Summary
Generally Speaking, based on the GDP change data, game marketing data, game sales data, and the game publisher sales data, we could see that for the different area, the game market and games sales has a lot of different. Japan has become the smallest market all over the world, and the USA and Europe have the similar size of the game market.

But, the game companies target the market as a whole global market, and this reduces the difference between game publishers. This can also somehow explain that the game market does not influence by the GDP too much, cause the publisher in this market is more influenced by the global preference, but not the preference or consumption habits from one country area.

From this, it leaves the inspiration that if a publisher would like to join this market, it is wise to use a global strategy to develop their companies. And if they have sufficient resource, it is better to design a game for long term market round, this benefit much more than those games has only a short life.
